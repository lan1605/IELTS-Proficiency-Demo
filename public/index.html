Features that we provide
For Student
Leverage data to accentuate student performance

For Teacher
Manage your institute with ease through smooth empanelment
Coordinate through our 4 panel systematic set-up: owner, trainer, student and content creator panels; ensuring efficient and easy coordination with rich user experience for every contributor.
Ease of payment with enhanced wallet features to reduce payment hassels, clubbed with logical trainer monitoring streamlines things for you as an owner.
Mentor Nex-Gen with Tech for an insightful guidance
With automated checking for reading and listening daily practice tasks and mocks, trainers can focus on quality mentoring. With digitised rectification of writing task responses, trainers can mark and correct any error a student makes whilst providing them with adequate reason. Speaking sessions can be conducted through video conferencing.
Break The Monotonous Routine
As a trainer, say no to repetitive tasks and reduce duplication of content across batches; month after month. For all the important information, upload it as either a blog post or an embedded youtube video for your students to view whenever they want to. Upload explainers for each writing task only once bidding adieu to repetition across batches.
Digitised Content Creator Panel For Streamlined Coordination
With the content creator panel, institutes can update teaching materials, daily practice task sheets, cue cards and everything with ease. With a scheduler for mock exams, upload your mock sheets once and schedule whenever necessary; bringing mindful productivity and better engagement.
Extensive Trainer Performance Tracking
As an institute owner, monitor the performance and efficiency of in-house teachers. Get detailed metrics like up-time, no. of writing tasks checked, speaking sessions conducted and more; that reduces idle time hence enhancing performance.
